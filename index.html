Raccoon Events

Private after-party events for friends and returning guests.

Check-in

Guests register at the door with a name and phone number, and confirm by replying Y to a one-time text message.

Messages

Registered guests occasionally receive event invitations, updates, photo links, and donation requests by SMS or WhatsApp — whichever they chose at registration. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe, or HELP for help.

How SMS opt-in works

Guests opt in in person at event check-in: staff ask for a mobile number, explain that Raccoon Events will send a confirmation text and occasional event-related messages, and note that consent is not a condition of entry. We send one confirmation message; only guests who reply Y are subscribed. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. See our SMS Terms and Privacy Policy. Phone numbers are never shared with third parties for marketing.

Donations

Guests are welcome to donate to the organizers via PayPal or Venmo. Any donations collected are used to improve future events and sustain them.
